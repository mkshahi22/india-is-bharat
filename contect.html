FAQ

How can I contribute content to the website?

If you have expertise in Indian history, culture, or any relevant field and would like to contribute, please fill out our contact form selecting "Content Contribution" as the subject. Our editorial team will review your proposal and get back to you.

I found incorrect information on your website. How can I report it?

We strive for accuracy in all our content. If you've found an error, please use our contact form with the subject "Factual Correction" and provide details about the error and the correct information. We appreciate your help in improving our content.

Do you accept guest posts?

Yes, we welcome guest contributions from experts in relevant fields. Please contact us with your expertise and proposed topic using our form above.

How can I stay updated with new content?

You can subscribe to our newsletter, follow us on social media, or create an account on our website to receive notifications about new content and updates.
